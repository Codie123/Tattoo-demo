Tattoo Mastery: From Artistry to Expertise

Introducing the comprehensive tattoo course offered by Tattoos Ahoy! Our program is meticulously crafted to provide aspiring tattoo artists with the knowledge and skills needed to embark on a successful journey in the world of tattooing. With a blend of theoretical understanding and hands-on practice, our course covers a wide range of essential topics.

From mastering the intricacies of tattoo machine types and their assembly to delving into the artistry of needle selection and stencil creation, our curriculum is designed to equip students with a deep understanding of the craft. We delve into the nuances of human skin structure, emphasizing its role in achieving stunning tattoo designs.

Tattoo Course Details

  • Comprehensive Curriculum: Delve into various aspects of tattooing, covering different types of tattoo machines, their components, and assembly techniques.

  • Needle Expertise: Explore the intricacies of tattoo needles, emphasizing their diverse applications and significance in the tattooing process.

  • Practical Skills Development: Hone practical skills through exercises in tattoo stencil creation using hectograph sheets.

  • Skin Structure Understanding: Gain an understanding of the human skin's structure and its relevance to tattooing, highlighting the significance of each layer in the process.

  • Precision Techniques: Master the art of setting up tattoo machines for different procedures and executing precise tattoo lines and color fills.

  • Creative Enhancement: Enhance creative abilities through classes dedicated to shading techniques and free-hand sketching, fostering individual style customization.
